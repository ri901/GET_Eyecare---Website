GET Eyecare seeks to promote Excellent Vision Everywhere. With this goal as a primary aim, to do this by ensuring that due diligence and optimal dedication is employed in all we do. We realise that our patients and partners are greatly important, hence we treat everyone as such.


Actively collaborating with colleagues and partners nationally and internationally via research report postings, community screening exercises, organised symposiums, Continuous Medical Education (C.M.E), exchange programmes and more.


Our ophthalmic consultations include obtaining relevant ophthalmological history, and conducting a detailed eye examination.


We provide or arrange for any further examinations, counselling, investigations, or treatment if required for our patient. These are usually done in a timescale that does not compromise patient safety and care.


We treat our patient with holistic lens on, hence we prescribe optical devices, drugs, or other treatment when we have adequate knowledge of the patient’s general health.


We provide effective patient care and treatments based on current good practice which is always aligned and in the best interests of the patient.


We only provide or recommend investigations, procedures and other treatments; whether drugs or optical devices only if these are clinically justified,


When in doubt, consult with professional colleagues appropriately for advice on assessment, examination, treatment and other aspects of patient care, We are always mindful to prioritise our patient’s confidentiality.
